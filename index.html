Boogie On Down
Welcome to Boogie On Down!
Collect the pellets to earn points! Collect the Disco Balls for even more points! Watch out for the guards trying to stop your dance party! Let's rock, baby!

Controls:
W/S: Move Up/Down
A/D: Move Left/Right
R: Reset
M: Toggle Music
1, 2, 3: Change Camera Angle
